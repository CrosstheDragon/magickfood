Rainbow Brew

A recipe for obtaining a rainbow brew to assist all kinds of magical activities1.

Note: This recipe is from The Complete book of Incense, Oils, and Brews by Scott Cunningham

Tools Needed

  • A nonmetallic pan or water catcher of some non-metallic sort
  • Bottle to store water in.
  • Something to label your bottle with.

Ingredients Needed

  • A rainy day
  • A rainbow

Cooking Insructions

  1. On a rainy day wait for the clouds to break somewhere and look for a rainbow.
  2. If you find one, put a saucer or some other nonmetallic pan outside where it can catch rain.
  3. If it rains while the rainbow is still present, save the water for ritual uses.
  4. It has been blessed by the rainbow's appearance. Because the rainbow contains all colors, this "brew" is useful for all types of magic.
  5. Bottle and label.
  6. Add to baths or anoint the body and hands while visualizing your magical goal.

1.This is not an edible recipe. At least, it's highly not recommended to do so.
